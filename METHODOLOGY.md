# Operational Governance & Delivery Methodology

## Overview
This methodology defines the strategic and operational framework for delivering complex, regulated Fintech and RWA (Real World Asset) programmes. It is built on 20+ years of delivery experience in banking and BFSI environments, ensuring that governance is a driver of success rather than a bottleneck.

## 1. The Core Philosophy: "Context-First" Delivery
Successful delivery in regulated environments is not about strictly following a single agile framework. It is about applying the right governance layer to the specific technical and regulatory context.

* **SAFe (Scaled Agile Framework):** Utilized at the enterprise and programme level to align strategy, budget, and complex dependencies across multiple teams and jurisdictions.
* **Scrum:** Applied at the development team level to maintain high-velocity feedback loops and clear sprint goals.
* **Kanban:** Leveraged for operational stability, DevOps flow, and managing compliance/security incidents where priorities shift rapidly.

## 2. The 7-Layer RWA Governance Model
Every programme is assessed and managed across seven distinct operational layers to ensure zero "blind spots":

* **Layer 0: Operating Model & Governance** – Defining roles (SC Engineers, MLRO, Key Ceremony Coordinators) and RACI matrices.
* **Layer 1: Regulatory & Compliance** – Embedding MiCA, DORA, and PSD2 requirements as delivery tasks, not just dependencies.
* **Layer 2: Asset & Legal** – Managing the legal lifecycle of tokenized assets.
* **Layer 3: Technical Architecture** – Overseeing API-heavy environments, cloud migrations (Azure/AWS), and monolith-to-microservices transitions.
* **Layer 4: Core Integration** – Managing deep integrations with systems like Temenos T24 and MuleSoft.
* **Layer 5: Ecosystem & Interoperability** – Coordinating multi-vendor environments and cross-border integrations.
* **Layer 6: Delivery Intelligence** – Utilizing AI-powered agents to automate reporting and risk escalation.

## 3. AI-Powered Delivery OS (Agentic Governance)
To eliminate manual overhead and human error in reporting, the methodology utilizes a browser-based, local-first AI agent architecture:

1.  **Discovery Agents:** Continuously monitor input signals from infrastructure (Azure/Datadog), core systems (T24), and team inputs (Jira/Slack).
2.  **Reasoning & Risk Agents:** Analyze signals against a pre-defined Knowledge Base of 60+ RWA risks and regulatory standards (DORA/MiCA).
3.  **Action Agents:** Automate executive reporting (Executive Flash), stakeholder notifications, and risk escalation paths.

## 4. Operational Reporting & Dashboards
Governance is visualized through a "Single Source of Truth" dashboard focusing on:
* **Gate Readiness:** Real-time tracking of country-specific go-live gates.
* **Integration Health:** Monitoring critical path dependencies across multi-country waves.
* **Risk Distribution:** High-level visualization of unmitigated vs. monitored risks.
