# RWA Infrastructure — 7-Layer Delivery Framework
**Delivery governance for regulated tokenisation programmes.**

Regulated RWA programmes operate at the intersection of blockchain infrastructure, securities law, custody obligations, and live compliance monitoring. Most fail not at the technology layer — but at Layer 0, before governance is defined.

This framework maps every layer a regulated RWA programme must address, in the sequence it must be addressed.

---

## The 7 Layers

| Layer | Focus | Key Risk if Skipped |
|---|---|---|
| L0 — Operating Model | Governance, RACI, decision rights | No one owns the hard decisions |
| L1 — Regulatory & Legal | MiCA, DORA, eWpG, BaFin licensing | Compliance becomes a blocker at L5 |
| L2 — Asset & Legal Structure | SPV, legal opinion, token classification | Token issuance blocked at gate |
| L3 — Technical Architecture | SC design, custody model, oracle selection | Architecture debt compounds at L5 |
| L4 — Build & Audit | SC development, pen test, SAST/DAST | Audit findings delay go-live |
| L5 — Integration & Testing | End-to-end, settlement, PoR baseline | Go-live with untested critical paths |
| L6 — Go-Live & Operations | G-OPS gate, hypercare, steady state | Operational blindness from Day 1 |
| L7 — Post-Launch & Steady State | Continuous compliance, KRI monitoring, reporting | Drift from regulatory posture goes undetected |
